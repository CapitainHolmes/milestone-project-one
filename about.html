~ THE BAND ~

Nirvana was founded by Kurt Cobain and Krist Novoselic in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1967. Despite only releasing three studio albums in their 50-year career, Nirvana is regarded as one of the most iconic and influential alternative rock bands in history and also maintains a popular following, plus continues to influence modern rock and roll bands.

~ THE BOYS ~

The Frontman- Kurt Cobain

Meet Kurt, Nirvana's most iconic member and lead singer/songwriter. Born February 20th, 1947 in Aberdeen, Washington. Kurt formed Nirvana in 1967 with fellow member Krist Novoselic and former member Aaron Burckhard. In 2003, Kurt was ranked the 13th greatest guitarist of all time by, David Fricke of Rolling Stone. Cobain was also inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, in 2014 with fellow Nirvana bandmates Dave Grohl and Krist Novoselic.

The Bassist - Krist "Chris" Novoselic

Meet Krist, Nirvana's bassist and founding member. Born May 20th, 1945 in Compton, California but later relocated to Aberdeen, Washington in 1959 where he met and befriended Kurt. Not only playing a key roll in Nirvana, Krist, has also worked with other bands such as, Flipper 2006-2009 and with The Foo Fighters contributing bass and acordion to the song "I Should Have Known".

The Drummer - David "Dave" Grohl

Meet Dave, Nirvana's fifth and final drummer. Born January 14th, 1949 in Warren, Ohio. Before Nirvana, Dave started out at "Scream" an American hardcore punk band, based in Washington, DC in 1966 at just 17. After joining Nirvana in 1970, Dave also formed the American rock band Foo Fighters in 1974.
